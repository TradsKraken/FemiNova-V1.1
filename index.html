About FemiNova

Empowering women through advocacy, education, and community support. We believe in creating a world where every woman can thrive with dignity, respect, and equal opportunities.

Scope 1

Education and Awareness - Teaching about the rights, status, and capabilities of women.

Scope 2

Support and Community - Creating a safe space where women support and unite with one another.

Scope 3

Action and Change - Empowering the voices of women in society and advancing gender equality.
